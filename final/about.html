About

We at Roots + Kin Studio invite you to collaborate with us in all of your creative projects. We are a young company, grounded in time-tested aesthetic principles while retaining the boldness and experimentation of youth. There are countless designers, artists, user experience experts, and content creators who are inhabiting and enriching our increasingly interconnected online world. At Roots + Kin we take a holisitic approach to aesthetics and creativity. Any artisitc endeavor exists within a complex social, cultural, and technological context. Whether your project is personal, professional, individual, collaborative, corporate, local, international, or cross-cultural we have the skills, tools and knowledge to help you realize your vision.

Roots + Kin Studio was founded in 2019 by artist and consultant Scott Kinyon. Scott has an extensive professional, educational and artistic background. Studies in Psychology, Organizational Leadership, Anthropology, Art, and Media Arts have equiped him with an understanding of artistic projects within their human contexts. Years spent as a Project Manager have provided him with the requisite tools and experience to manage complex implementations within the practical constraints of time, budget, and other resources. He has a passion for listening to and understanding others, which serves as an essential foundation for all work.
