Services

Roots + Kin Studio offers a multitude of services and our portfolio is ever increasing. We are especially excited to take on new, innovative and complex projects. We believe in learning, growing and collaborating with you to accomplish your artistic, creative or communication goals. What follows is a short, and by no means comprehensive, list of some of our passions and specialties.

Branding

A lot goes into branding. If you are running a business, then you know how important it is to communicate your identity to your customers and stakeholders. While we at Roots + Kin Studio can help with logo design, web design, and corporate style guides we understand that these are all just pieces of something much larger. We can help you identify, refine and express your desired corporate image and culture.

Content

The world today craves content. The amount of information and media we consume on a daily basis is astronomically greater than even a few decades ago. Amidst the noise of a technocratic world ruled by technology and algorithms how do you create and deliver meaningful content? We at Roots + Kin Studio can help! We understand that the glut of content available now can lead to content indigestion as users rush from one product or story to another. We are not only well-versed in the technical skills to create narrative and media content, but we also understand the psychological and anthropological factors that set meaningful, digestable content apart from the noise of the times.

Consulting

Sometimes your needs go beyond rebranding and content creation. We at Roots + Kin understand the value of long-term collaboration. Some narrative and artisitc endeavors are large enough in scope and lengthy enough in duration that you need partners you trust to guide you. We offer comprehensive consulting services ranging from corporate culture analysis and design to art direction for longterm media series. And lest we forget to mention it, we are always up for something new. Our holistic, renaissance approach to creative problem-solving puts us in a prime position to partner with you on a wide range of projects.
