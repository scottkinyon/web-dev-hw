Chickpea Pasta

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas - 1 can
  • Pasta - 1 box
  • Black Olives - 1 can
  • Tomatoes - 1 can or package
  • Onion - 1 whole
  • Kale - 1-2 stalks
  • Garlic - 3-5 cloves
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Oregano
  • CRP
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine

Directions

  1. Fill saucepan with water over high heat.
  2. Fill bottom of cast iron skillet with thin layer of olive oil, turn to medium heat.
  3. Open can of chickpeas, rinse with water and drain.
  4. Empty can of chickpeas into cast iron, stir occasionally.
  5. While chickpeas cook, dice onion and garlic cloves.
  6. Add diced onion and garlic to cast iron with a few dashes of salt, black pepper, crp and oregano.
  7. By now water in saucepan should be boiling, add pasta to saucepan.
  8. Open can of olives and slice.
  9. By now onions should be somewhat translucent. Add kale and tomatoes to cast iron along with a hearty splash of red wine.
  10. Stir contents of cast iron occasionally until pasta is done.
  11. Strain pasta, remove from heat.
  12. Add sliced black olives to cast iron, stir until warm. Add additional salt, black pepper, oregano and crp to taste.
